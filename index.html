Important Games in My Life

The Zelda Series

I grew up with this series and have always looked at the developers of these games for inspiration in my own ideas. I grew up with this series and I have nothing but fond memories of playing and enjoying these games.

Fortnite

I hated this game when I played it and I hate it even more now. But, I also love this game as it connected me with all of my friends today and I played all the time with my best friend, and we wouldn't be as close today if it wasn't for the literal days we have spent on this game.

Death Stranding

This game was met with mixed reviews, some saying it was a masterpiece of 2019, some saying it wasn't even a game. I liked this game and I loved it for it's creativity and I also credit it with giving me ideas for new games.

My Career Choices

Game Designer

My whole life I have played and enjoyed video games so I would enjoy being able to create the same experiences for people.

Theoretical Physicist

I honestly don't know much about this field other than they work with subjects we know little to no info about other than that it exists. I think being able to discover new facts about space time or the creation of the universe would be fun and unique than any other career.

Movie Director

Another career I don't know much about, making a movie is, in a way, similar to making a game. You have to have all the elements of the movie combined and orchestrated perfectly or it will fail. I want to create experiences that people won't forget and creating movies is one way I could do that.
